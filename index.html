Enjoy culture, Shopping, and Cuisines in Rotterdam

Top three activities to do at Rotterdam

Go for a walk in our Zoo: Blijdorp in Rotterdam

Blijdorp is a great place for the family to see different Zoo animals

Go for a 'picnic' in the Markthal Rotterdam

Enjoy the greatest foodcourt, and cuisines at the Rotterdam Markthal, which is located next to trainstation Rotterdam Blaak

Go for great heights when visiting the Euromast Rotterdam

Enjoy the scenery and skyline of Rotterdam, when visiting the Euromast. You can also enjoy some delicious lunch or dinner with the family.
